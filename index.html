FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a Notion Certified Consultancy do?

A Notion Certified Consultancy provides expert guidance on designing, implementing, and optimizing Notion-based operating systems. We handle everything from workflow discovery and architecture to migration, automation, training, and ongoing support — ensuring your systems are built for scale.

How long does implementation take?

Timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A focused workflow implementation may take 2–4 weeks, while enterprise-wide transformations typically span 8–16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase.

Can you migrate from existing tools?

Yes. We specialize in secure migrations from platforms including Asana, Monday.com, Airtable, Confluence, spreadsheets, and legacy databases — preserving data structure, relationships, and historical context.

Do you provide team training?

Absolutely. Custom training programs are a core part of our delivery framework. We equip teams with practical knowledge through role-based sessions, documentation, and hands-on workshops.

Can you build custom automations?

Yes. We design and implement automations using Notion's native capabilities alongside integrations with Zapier, Make, and custom API connections to eliminate manual processes.

Do you offer ongoing support?

We provide continuous optimization, system maintenance, and operational guidance through structured support packages tailored to your organization's needs.
